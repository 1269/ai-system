# Getting Started — Your First AI System

> Companion guide to [Video 1: I'm Building My Own AI System — Here's Why You Should Too](https://youtube.com/@jashia)

## The Problem

Every time you open ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool, you start from zero. It doesn't know your name. It doesn't know what you're working on. It doesn't remember that thing you figured out together last week.

You're training the AI to help you — then throwing that training away at the end of every session.

## The Fix

An AI system is a set of files that give any AI tool three things:

1. **Context** — who you are, how you work, what you're building
2. **Memory** — what the AI has learned about you over time
3. **Rituals** — daily habits that keep you and your AI aligned

Three files. Fifteen minutes to set up. And from that point on, every AI session builds on the last one.

## Quick Start

### Option 1: Use the Starter Kit (Recommended)

The [AI System Starter Kit](../starter-kit/AI-SYSTEM-STARTER.md) is a single prompt that walks you through the entire setup interactively. It works with any AI tool.

1. Open the [Starter Kit](../starter-kit/AI-SYSTEM-STARTER.md)
2. Copy the prompt
3. Paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any LLM
4. Answer the questions
5. Save the files it generates

### Option 2: Use the Templates

If you'd rather fill things in manually, grab the templates:

- [CONTEXT.md](../templates/CONTEXT.md) — Tell AI who you are
- [MEMORY.md](../templates/MEMORY.md) — Give AI a place to store what it learns
- [MORNING-CHECKIN.md](../templates/rituals/MORNING-CHECKIN.md) — A 2-minute daily ritual

## How to Use Your System

### Starting a new AI conversation

Paste your CONTEXT.md at the beginning, or attach it as a file if your AI tool supports that. The AI will immediately know who you are and how to help you.

### After an important session

Open MEMORY.md and add what you learned — decisions made, problems solved, preferences discovered. This is how your AI gets smarter over time.

### Every morning

Run the Morning Check-In prompt. It reads your context and memory, reminds you what you're working on, and helps you pick your top 3 priorities. Two minutes, every day.

## What's Next

Once your system is running, you can add layers:

- **More rituals** — evening shutdown, weekly review
- **Skills** — reusable prompts for tasks you repeat
- **Integrations** — connect AI to your calendar, email, and notes
- **Voice** — talk to your AI system instead of typing

Don't add these yet. Use the basics for a week first. Then come back and add what you actually need.
